* **Score:** **9.0 / 10**
* **Comments:** The problem is clearly identified and validated through a real-world case study at the CS Faculty Library (HCMUS). This represents a genuine, high-pain problem backed by quantitative data.

### 2. Market Need
* **Score:** **8.5 / 10**
* **Comments:** Highly urgent need across all three key stakeholder groups: Librarians (reducing manual data entry burden), Students (instant 24/7 access), and Management (real-time ROI dashboards). The solution directly targets core user pain points.

### 3. Market Size & Reach

* **Comments:** Although starting at faculty level, the automated digitization solution with Vietnamese OCR capability can scale to hundreds of universities, colleges, and public libraries across Vietnam. Future iterations should further outline commercialization roadmaps outside internal deployment.

### 4. Business Impact
* **Score:** **9.0 / 10**
* **Comments:** Quantitative Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are explicit: 70% reduction in processing time, reduction in student access delay from days to under 5 seconds, and minimal illegal file leakage due to Canvas Reader DRM. Long-term impact enhances student academic performance and accelerates educational digital transformation.

### 5. Business Viability
* **Score:** **8.5 / 10**
* **Comments:** 
  * Initial Development Budget: **90,500,000 VND** (for 6 engineers over 8 weeks) is highly realistic and feasible for a faculty-level project.
  * Annual Operating Budget: **70,440,000 VND/year** (AWS Cloud infra at 28.44M VND/year) is cost-effective compared to commercial software licenses (125M – 1.25B VND/year).

### 6. Technical Feasibility & Complexity
* **Score:** **9.0 / 10**
* **Comments:** 
  * Vietnamese OCR engines (VietOCR & PaddleOCR) are mature with 95-98% accuracy on standard printed books.
  * Architecture decoupling Web Server and Background OCR Workers via Redis BullMQ queue eliminates OOM crashes on heavy PDF files.
  * DRM security via Canvas Reader and AWS Presigned URLs offers a smart, feasible technical approach without expensive DRM licensing.

### 7. Competitive Advantage / Moat
* **Score:** **7.0 / 10**
* **Comments:** Core competitive edge lies in deep optimization for Vietnamese diacritics and tailored workflows for Vietnamese libraries. Moat is driven by user experience (UX) refinement and rapid deployment capabilities.

### 8. Growth Potential
* **Score:** **8.0 / 10**
* **Comments:** Aligns with Prime Ministerial Decision 206/QĐ-TTg regarding national library digital transformation mandates. LIBIF holds strong potential for expansion as a SaaS model or technology transfer package.

### 9. Risk Assessment
* **Score:** **7.5 / 10**
* **Comments:** Good identification of timeline and adoption risks. Copyright compliance risks are addressed technically by DRM Canvas Reader restricting raw file downloads and enforcing authenticated access.

### 10. Evidence Confidence
* **Score:** **8.5 / 10**
* **Comments:** Baseline estimates for manual processing times, student demand, and infrastructure costs are grounded in realistic local market data for 2026. High evidence confidence.
